# Navbar Dropdown Regression Checklist

## Desktop

1. Open the homepage and hover `Localities`, `Services`, and `Resources`.
2. Confirm each submenu appears below the header and is not clipped.
3. Click each dropdown trigger and confirm the submenu stays open without hover.
4. Click outside the navbar and confirm the open submenu closes.
5. Open a submenu, press `Escape`, and confirm it closes.
6. Resize until items move into `More`, then open `More` and verify its links are visible and clickable.

## Mobile

1. Open the mobile menu.
2. Tap each dropdown parent and confirm it expands and collapses.
3. Tap a submenu link and confirm navigation occurs and the mobile menu closes.
4. Re-open the menu and confirm no submenu remains stuck open from the previous route.

## Variants

1. Repeat the desktop checks on a page using `dark-header has-border`.
2. Confirm the chevron remains visible and rotates correctly in both light and dark headers.
